For PowerShell:

Explanation of SoX Parameters used:

    -r 8000: Resamples the audio to 8000 Hz (8 kHz).
    -c 1: Mixes down any multi-channel audio (like stereo) into a single mono channel.
    -b 8: Sets the output bit depth to 8 bits.
    -e u-law: Specifies the CCITT u-law encoding, commonly used for telephony and IVR systems.
    "converted/$file.Name": Places the processed files into a subfolder to prevent overwriting your original high-quality source files.
